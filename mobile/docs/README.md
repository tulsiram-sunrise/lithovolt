## ðŸ†˜ Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Privacy Policy URL Not Accessible
**Solution**: Ensure your website is live and URL is publicly accessible (not behind login)

### Issue: Screenshots Rejected
**Solution**: Ensure screenshots show actual app content, not mockups or marketing materials

### Issue: App Icon Too Similar to Others
**Solution**: Review competitors and make your icon more distinctive

### Issue: Description Contains Prohibited Content
**Solution**: Remove pricing, comparisons to competitors, or promotional language

### Issue: Age Rating Issues
**Solution**: Accurately disclose all app content, especially user-generated content

## ðŸŽ“ Pro Tips

### 1. Start Early
Begin preparing assets at least 2-3 weeks before planned launch

### 2. Test Thoroughly
The #1 reason for rejection is crashes or broken functionality

### 3. Screenshots Sell
Users spend 90% of time looking at screenshots - make them count!

### 4. Optimize Description
Use keywords naturally in the description (especially first 2-3 sentences)

### 5. Respond to Reviews
Always respond to user reviews within 24-48 hours, especially negative ones

### 6. Update Regularly
Plan to update every 4-6 weeks with bug fixes and improvements

### 7. Monitor Competitors
See what's working for similar apps in your category

### 8. A/B Test
After launch, test different screenshots, icons, and descriptions
